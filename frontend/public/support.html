Access and account help

Accounts for Trident Intelligence are provisioned as part of the underlying business service arrangement with Trident Virtual. If you cannot sign in, need account access, or require role changes, contact product support using the email above.

Technical assistance

The app provides AI-assisted, source-linked responses for vessel-support workflows. If you notice an issue with chat responses, cited sources, manuals, or document access, please include the relevant session title or document name in your support request.

Trident Intelligence and all AI-generated guidance are advisory only and must be verified against official manuals, procedures, and operational requirements.
